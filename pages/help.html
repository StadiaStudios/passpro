Welcome to PassPro! Here’s how to get the most out of your new password manager.

🗝️ Getting Started

  • All your passwords and notes are stored securely on your device with strong encryption—no cloud required.
  • From the home page, unlock your vault and you’re good to go!

⚙️ Settings

  • Visit Settings to customize PassPro, change your master password, back up, or import/export your data.
  • Use Export Data and Import Data for safe transfer or backup on your device or drive.
  • Device backup (e.g. iCloud, Android Backup) includes PassPro data on most browsers.

🔑 Reset & Troubleshooting

  • If you forget your master password, you can reset your vault from Settings. (All content inside your vault will be erased for security.)
  • Contact support if you have issues migrating from Classic PassPro, or for help with lost data/backups.

💡 More Tips

  • For syncing across devices, use Settings > Export/Import as needed, or rely on your browser's own backup system.
  • Curious about privacy? PassPro never uploads your data or secrets. Everything is handled locally unless you export somewhere yourself.
