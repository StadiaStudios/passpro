Welcome to PassPro! Here’s how to get the most out of your new password manager.

🗝️ Getting Started

  • All your passwords and notes are stored securely on your device with strong encryption—no cloud required.
  • From the home page, unlock your vault and you’re good to go!

📲 How to Install the App

  • On Desktop (Chrome, Edge, Brave): Look for the install icon (a computer with a down arrow) in your browser’s address bar, or open your browser’s menu and select “Install PassPro”.
  • On Android (Chrome): Tap the three dots menu > “Install app”. The app will appear on your home screen and app drawer for quick access.
  • On iPhone/iPad (Safari): Tap the Share icon, then choose “Add to Home Screen” to create a quick-launch icon just like a native app.
  • Once installed, PassPro data saves with app instead of Safari Data and it's more protected!

🔐 How to Use the Password Generator

  • In New PassPro (recommended):
    1. Go to Password Generator from the main menu or sidebar.
    2. Choose the desired password length using the slider.
    3. Toggle which types of characters to include (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols, or even custom keywords).
    4. Click the “Generate” button. Your new password instantly appears above!
    5. Click the copy button to copy the generated password, or generate again for a new one. You can view password strength and your generator history as well.
    6. Pro Tip: The new generator dynamically shows strength and lets you adjust options for your needs!
  • Using Classic PassPro Generator:
    1. Go to the Classic Generator if you prefer the old style.
    2. Select your options (length, types, keywords) as desired.
    3. Click “Generate” and copy your password as needed.
    4. Note: The classic version’s UI is a bit different, but all basic functions work the same way. Strength indicators and history are also included!

You can use either version, but we recommend the new generator for more features and a more modern experience.

💾 How to Import/Export Your Account

  • Export Your Account Data:
    1. Go to Settings in PassPro.
    2. Find the Export Data option.
    3. Choose your preferred format: .json file for standard backup or Disguise PNG for an image-based encrypted backup.
    4. After exporting, store the file somewhere safe, such as a USB drive, external storage, or a secure cloud platform.
  • Import Data to Restore or Transfer Your Account:
    1. On a new device, or after resetting, go to Settings > Import Data.
    2. Choose your exported .json or Disguise PNG backup file.
    3. Follow the prompts to complete the import. Your vault will be restored—existing data may be overwritten, so make sure you've backed up recent changes.
  • Tips for Import/Export:
    • Always keep your backup files private and secure. Anyone with access to your export file could potentially access your data.
    • If you use multiple devices, repeat the Export/Import process to keep them in sync.
    • For switching between Classic and New PassPro, export/import features are compatible, but some advanced features in the new version might not transfer perfectly to Classic.

⚙️ Settings

  • Visit Settings to customize PassPro, change your master password, back up, or import/export your data.
  • Use Export Data and Import Data for safe transfer or backup on your device or drive.
  • Device backup (e.g. iCloud, Android Backup) includes PassPro data on most browsers.

🔑 Reset & Troubleshooting

  • If you forget your master password, you can reset your vault from Settings. (All content inside your vault will be erased for security.)
  • Contact support if you have issues migrating from Classic PassPro, or for help with lost data/backups.

🐞 How to Report a Bug

  • If you notice something isn’t working quite right or find a bug, we appreciate your help in making PassPro better!
  • Step 1: Take note of what you were doing when the issue happened (which page, which device/browser, any steps to reproduce).
  • Step 2: If you can, include screenshots or error messages. This helps us fix the bug faster!
  • Step 3: Email all the details to stadiastudios.support@proton.me. We review all reports and will get back to you as soon as possible.
  • Optional: We also have a Google Form that is more likely to be reviewed as well here: Report Bug Form

🔒 Forgot PIN / Locked Out?

  • If you've forgotten your PIN or have been locked out of the app, you still have options!
  • Try unlocking with your PIN First: Try unlocking with your PIN first. If you fail after a couple attempts, move to step 2.
  • Search for a Recovery Backup: The new Export as Disguise PNG function in settings allows you to backup a blank image as data or your .json file. If you've backed up your data before, try searching again.
  • Permanently locked out? If you cannot remember your master password and no other recovery is available, you can reset your vault from the Settings or Login page. This will erase all existing data for security reasons but allow you to set a new password and PIN.
  • Need help backing up before a reset or have another issue? Contact Support for personal assistance. But remember, as mentioned in the TOS, we are not responsible for any data loss.

↩️ How to Revert to Classic PassPro

  • If you prefer using Classic PassPro, you can return to it any time.
  • Step 1: Go to the Classic PassPro site.
  • Step 2: If you've exported your data from the new PassPro, use the import function in Classic PassPro to restore your vault. Be sure to back up any important data before reverting.
  • Note: Some features or data formats in the new PassPro may not be supported exactly the same way in Classic PassPro. However, some updates may come for bug fixes and minor improvements. View separate changelog here

💡 More Tips

  • You can back up your passwords and data by exporting your vault (as a .json file or Disguise PNG) and saving it to a USB drive, external storage, or your file manager for safe keeping.
